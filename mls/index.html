Welcome to the interactive laboratory for the MLS course. These tools are designed to provide intuition about fundamental Machine Learning concepts.

  • Decision Trees Recursive Partitioning Lab Interactively split Temperature and Rainfall space to understand how CART algorithms minimize impurity.
  • Representation Learning Recursive Partitioning Lab Interactively learn a representation space for high-dimensional objects like images.
